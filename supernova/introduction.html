Get started

  • Our design system is specifically designed for designers and developers to enable both teams to create and engineer beautiful, on-brand applications and solutions quickly. Each section and page specified in our design and development document includes both English and Japanese explanation. For pages specified for designers, a member of the creative team can quickly find specified components from the provided Figma libraries and related visual resources. For pages specified for developers, an engineering team member can quickly find selected libraries and codes for defined components for their project.
